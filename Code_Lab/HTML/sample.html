My Travel Bucket List

Destinations:

  • Paris, France
  • Santorini. Greece
  • Tokyo, Japan
  • Machu Picchu, Peru
  • Maui, Hawaii
  • Grand Canyon, Arizona
  • Rome, Italy

Reasons to Visit:

  • Explore the Eiffel Tower and indulge in French cuisine in Paris, France.
  • Relax on the stunning beaches and enjoy the iconic white-washed buildings in Santorini, Greece.
  • Experience the vibrant culture, delicious food, and advanced technology in Tokyo, Japan.
  • Embark on a hike to witness the breathtaking views of Machu Picchu, Peru.
  • Enjoy snorkeling, sunbathing, and breathtaking sunsets in Maui, Hawaii.
  • Marvel at the grandeur of the Grand Canyon's natural beauty in Arizona, USA.
  • Step back in time and explore ancient ruius and Roman history in Rome, Italy
